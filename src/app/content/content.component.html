1. Zoosk


A Diverse Dating Platform That Caters to Every Preference Zoosk


Free Version: Yes

One month's cost of Zoosk's subscription: From $10/month

Number of members: 40 million Zoosk emerges as an inclusive, user-friendly dating app catering to a diverse audience, including the LGBTQ+ community. It's particularly beneficial for gay individuals seeking various connections, from serious relationships to casual encounters. With over 40 million users and 30 million app downloads, Zoosk offers a vast pool of potential matches. Its standout features include a Behavioral Matchmaking™ system, SmartPick introductions, and a unique Carousel feature for discovering new profiles.

The app's intuitive interface, comprehensive search filters, and options for connecting via messaging and virtual gifts make it a preferred choice. Despite its wide range of features, full access requires a subscription, but you can get a feel for it with a limited free account.

Why we chose Zoosk: We chose Zoosk for its commitment to creating a safe and inclusive environment for the LGBTQ+ community. Zoosk's robust security measures include a reliable reporting system that allows users to report harassment, hate speech, or inappropriate behavior. Each report is taken seriously, with actions ranging from warnings to suspensions or banning of offending accounts. Zoosk ensures a secure and tailored dating experience.

Our experience with Zoosk: Our experience with Zoosk was positively influenced by its smart matchmaking algorithm. This technology effectively analyzes user interactions to suggest highly compatible matches, making it a standout choice for the LGBTQ+ community.
2. SilverSingles


A Place for Gay People Over 50 to Easily Find Other Singles


Free Version: Yes

One month's cost of ZSingles's subscription: From $10/month

It’s not always easy meeting gay singles over 50, but SilverSingles makes it comfortable and easy. This dating site allows LGBTQ+ individuals of all ages to sign up and meet a potential match, by using a matchmaking algorithm that pairs you with singles according to your individual preferences.

It doesn’t matter if you identify as gay, queer, bisexual, or any other orientation, SilverSingles will provide you with a safe, curated dating platform. A basic free membership will let you create a profile, see daily match suggestions, and find a potential date on the site with the search options. You can also send out likes and smiles to show your interest in someone on the site. While the basic free membership of SilverSingles allows profile creation and daily match suggestions, it limits communication options and detailed profile viewing. Upgrading to a paid subscription unlocks full communication tools, more detailed profile insights, and access to a more comprehensive list of matches, enhancing the overall dating experience and chances of finding a compatible partner.

Why we chose Catering specifically to mature singles who are 50 and older, SilverSingles provides a safe and receptive platform for LGBTQ+ elders to find love and companionship. The in-depth personality test ensures compatible matches, helping to create meaningful connections.

Our experience with SilverSingles: Our experience on SilverSingles is that the platform offers a mix of positives and challenges. While we value responsive customer service and the excitement of encountering new individuals, we also face issues with the accuracy of match suggestions. It's a journey of varied experiences, reflecting the diverse nature of the user base.
3. Gaystryst


For Gay and Bi-Sexual Men Looking for Casual Dates


Free Version: Yes

One month's cost of subscription: From $10/month

Gaystryst is a dating website for men looking for fun and casual hookups with other men. Unlike other gay dating apps and sites, this one doesn’t include women at all - it's for gay guys only. The majority of users are from the U.S. and between the ages of mid-20s to mid-30s, but you can find plenty of men of all ages. Using filters to find your match and sending winks is free. However, if you want to send messages, the main form of communication on Gaystryst, you'll have to pay, as this is a feature only available to premium members.

Why we chose As an app specifically designed for gay singles, Gaystryst offers a safe and comfortable space for men to connect with like-minded individuals. It places emphasis on safety and privacy, making it an ideal choice for hassle-free gay dating.

Our experience with GaysTryst: On one hand, we appreciate its user-friendly interface and the opportunities it presents for connecting with gay singles. On the other hand, we've encountered challenges with promotional tactics throughout the app experience.
4. HER


The Dating and Social App for Queer Women


Free Version: Yes

One month's cost of Her's subscription: From $10/month

Over the years, the pioneer in lesbian dating apps HER has developed into an all-inclusive platform for women and femmes of all types, identities, and sexual preferences. HER embraces and welcomes more than 18 gender identities and 17 sexual orientations on the profile selection list. HER’s app is easy to use, loaded with communication features, and even hosts local events for more community building.

Why we chose HER We included HER on our list for its commitment to providing an inclusive platform for LGBTQ+ women. The app goes beyond dating, offering connections for friendship, networking, and discussions on queer news and events.

Our experience with Her: We loved HER’s "Communities" features. This unique aspect allows users to join smaller community groups within the app based on their interests or identities. For example, there are communities for different identities and interests like "Trans Womxn," "Queer Womxn of Color," "Bi Womxn," and more, allowing users to connect on more specific, shared experiences. Additionally, HER's event feature, which lists LGBTQ+ events in the user's area and worldwide, is another standout aspect. This feature encourages real-life meetups and community engagement, extending the app's utility beyond online interactions. We really liked that HER has features that make it more than just a dating app; they transform it into a social hub for LGBTQ+ women and queer individuals, emphasizing community building and shared experiences.
